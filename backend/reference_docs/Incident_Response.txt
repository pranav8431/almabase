LedgerShield follows a formal incident response lifecycle with defined stages.
The stages are preparation, detection, triage, containment, eradication, recovery, and post-incident review.
Security monitoring systems generate alerts continuously across production services.
Potential incidents are triaged by the security team within 30 minutes of detection.
Confirmed high-severity incidents are escalated immediately to the incident commander.
Initial containment actions are initiated within 1 hour for high-severity incidents.
Customers impacted by a confirmed security incident are notified within 24 hours.
Regulatory notification obligations are assessed by legal and compliance teams during triage.
LedgerShield has a dedicated security team responsible for incident response operations.
The security team includes incident commanders, forensic analysts, and communications leads.
All incident actions are logged in a centralized incident management platform.
Evidence is preserved according to forensic handling procedures.
A post-incident review is completed within 5 business days after incident closure.
The post-incident review includes root cause analysis and control gap identification.
Corrective actions are assigned owners, due dates, and verification criteria.
Lessons learned are incorporated into runbooks, monitoring rules, and training updates.
