LedgerShield maintains an active SOC 2 Type II attestation.
The SOC 2 Type II scope includes security, availability, and confidentiality controls.
LedgerShield maintains ISO 27001 certification for its information security management system.
ISO 27001 control ownership is documented and reviewed by compliance management.
An accredited third-party audit firm performs annual external audits.
Audit findings are tracked in a formal corrective action register.
Compliance policies are reviewed every 12 months.
Material regulatory or business changes trigger out-of-cycle policy updates.
All employees complete mandatory security awareness training during onboarding.
All employees complete annual refresher security training.
Engineering and operations personnel complete role-specific secure operations training.
Training completion records are maintained in the learning management system.
Vendors handling sensitive data are evaluated through a risk assessment process.
Internal control testing is performed quarterly by the compliance and security teams.
Executive leadership reviews compliance status reports every quarter.
Non-compliance issues are assigned owners and remediation deadlines.
